## What I do on my blog

This is my blog, a non-profit and completely ad-free personal website.

It runs on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/), so it's hosted on one of the [GitHub](https://github.com/)'s servers. The blog itself is a 100% static website, and I don't have any backend for serving the content or collecting any information about its visitors.

However, I'm using third-party products to deliver certain functionalities. These products have their own cookies and privacy policy.

- For comments, I'm using [Disqus](https://disqus.com). You might want to read their [privacy policy](https://help.disqus.com/en/articles/1717103-disqus-privacy-policy)
- For traffic analysis, I'm using [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/). Microsoft presents [this document](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement) as a privacy policy they use.

The only thing this website stores in your browser is your choice about the cookies.
